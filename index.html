NotifySync is a simple application for integrating your computer and smartphone with the following functionality:

  • Deliver notifications from your phone to your computer (including support for actions inside notifications)
  • Send and receive files over network
  • Check phone battery level
  • Find device (ever if all sounds disabled)
  • Display incoming call
  • Shared clipboard
  • Strong communications encryption
  • Works over Wi-Fi
